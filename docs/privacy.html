Privacy Policy

Safari Enter Guard does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The extension runs locally in Safari pages to detect CJK IME composition events and Enter key events. It uses this information only to prevent accidental form submission while text conversion is being confirmed.

The extension requests access to all websites because accidental Enter submission can happen on any website with text input. Page content, typed text, browsing history, account information, and form data are not sent to any server.

The app does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, third-party SDKs, or remote logging.
